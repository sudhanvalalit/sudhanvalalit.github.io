# Superburst and X-Ray Bursts

![X-Ray Burst](../img/computation.png)

X-ray bursts and superbursts are thermonuclear explosions on the surfaces of accreting neutron stars. These phenomena provide unique laboratories for testing nuclear physics under extreme conditions.
