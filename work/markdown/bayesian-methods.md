# Bayesian Methods

![Bayesian Analysis](../img/NuclearAstro/Supernova.jpeg)

Bayesian inference provides a powerful statistical framework for analyzing nuclear data and making predictions with quantified uncertainties. My work applies Bayesian methods to extrapolate nuclear properties, constrain theoretical models, and explore uncharted regions of the nuclear chart.
