# Dense Matter Equations of State

![EoS Plot](../img/NuclearAstro/TvsP2log.jpg)

Understanding the equation of state (EoS) of dense nuclear matter is crucial for modeling neutron stars and core-collapse supernovae. My research focuses on developing realistic EoS models that accurately describe matter at extreme densities.
