# Emulators

![Emulation](../img/Computation/comp-bg.jpeg)

Emulators are fast, accurate surrogate models that replace expensive computations with machine learning-based approximations. My research develops emulation techniques for astrophysical calculations, enabling rapid parameter space exploration and Bayesian inference.
