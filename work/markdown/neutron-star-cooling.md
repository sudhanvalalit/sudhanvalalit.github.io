# Neutron Star Cooling

![Neutron Star](../img/NuclearAstro/Supernova.jpeg)

Neutron stars cool through various mechanisms including neutrino emission and photon radiation from the surface. Understanding these cooling processes provides constraints on the interior composition and equation of state.
