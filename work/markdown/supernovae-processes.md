# Neutrino processes

![Supernova Explosion](../img/NuclearAstro/Supernova.jpeg)

Core-collapse supernovae are among the most energetic events in the universe. My research investigates the nuclear physics processes that govern supernova dynamics and nucleosynthesis, with a focus on neutrino physics and weak interaction processes.
