# Student Mentorship & Educational Programs

## Student Advising

- **Graduate Student Advising** - Supervising graduate students on research projects in nuclear astrophysics, computational physics, and Bayesian methods, from problem formulation through publication.
- **Undergraduate Student Advising** - Mentoring undergraduate researchers through independent study and research projects, including their first experience with scientific computing and data analysis.

## Research Mentorship

- **Undergraduate Research Projects** - Guiding students through independent research
- **Graduate Student Supervision** - PhD thesis supervision
- **Postdoc Mentoring** - Career development and research guidance

## Educational Workshops

- **"Introduction to Computational Physics"** - Python programming for physics
- **"Data Analysis in Nuclear Physics"** - Statistical methods and uncertainty quantification
- **"Research Skills Development"** - Scientific writing and presentation skills
- **"Version Control for Scientists"** - Git and collaborative research tools

## Apply for Mentorship

Interested undergraduate and graduate students can reach out to discuss research opportunities and mentorship programs.
